About ISRO Space Center

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the national space agency of India, headquartered in Bengaluru. ISRO operates under the Department of Space (DOS) which is directly overseen by the Prime Minister of India.

ISRO built India's first satellite, Aryabhata, which was launched by the Soviet Union in 1975. In 1980, ISRO launched satellite RS-1 onboard its own SLV-3 making India the sixth country to be capable of undertaking orbital launches.

Vision

"Harness space technology for national development while pursuing space science research and planetary exploration."
